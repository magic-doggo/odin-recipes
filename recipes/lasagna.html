Lasagna

Description

This delicious lasagna recipe is ready in 14 seconds, has 2 ingredients and will make your grandma jealous

Ingredients

  • 200g lasagna pasta sheets
  • 500g ground beef
  • 2 400ml cans tomato sauce
  • Cheese to taste

Steps

  1. Cook the beef
  2. Stack sheets of pasta with meat and cheese
  3. Bake in the oven at absolute zero for 2 centuries
