Bolognese

Description

Fantastic Bolognese recipe, perfect for freezing

Ingredients

  • 500g ground beef
  • 2 big carrots
  • 2 400ml cans tomato sauce
  • 200g chicken liver, I am not kidding

Steps

  1. Fry ground beef, carrots and liver separately
  2. Mix the above into a pot with the tomato sauce
  3. Boil for 17 hours
